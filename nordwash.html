About Nordwash

Nordwash is a modern laundry solution for busy Nigerians who value convenience, transparency, and trust. The app lets users schedule pickups, customize fabric care, track laundry progress in real time, and pay securely—all through a smooth mobile experience.

The Problem

Many laundry users in Nigeria—especially in cities like Lagos—feel stuck with unreliable, outdated services. They deal with frequent delays, inconsistent pricing, missing items, and little to no communication. Most providers don't let customers give fabric-specific instructions or track orders.

My Goal

Design a mobile experience that directly addresses the trust, communication, and convenience gaps in Nigeria's laundry market. The aim: an app that feels dependable and personal, from scheduling to delivery.

UX Research

I interviewed residential users (students, professionals, homemakers) and commercial clients (hotels, salons, gyms) to understand laundry behaviors, fears, and expectations.

Key Insights

  • Trust and reliability are more important than speed.
  • Users want real-time updates to avoid being kept in the dark.
  • Transparent pricing and service breakdowns build credibility.
  • Custom preferences for detergent, starch, and care are crucial.
  • Communication (via chat or call) makes users feel more in control.

What I Learned

This case study reinforced that in service-based industries like laundry, UX is less about flashy design and more about earning trust. Real-time feedback, customization, reliability, and transparency are nonnegotiables. I also learned the value of grounding every design decision in real user fears and needs, because what feels minor in design can create major discomfort in daily life.
