NORDWASH

A Nigeria-based laundry application.

My Role

Product Designer

Tools

Figma & Chat GPT

Timeline

3 Weeks

Tasks

Design & Research

Team

None

The Problems

  1. Laundry services often lack transparency and convenience, hurting user experience and retention.
  2. Clothes are sometimes returned damaged due to poor handling.
  3. Pricing isn't clearly communicated upfront.
  4. Users don't get real-time updates on laundry progress or delivery.
  5. There's no flexibility in how fast or what type of service users can select.
  6. There's no history of past orders or a way to give real-time feedback or ratings.

How I Solved Them

  1. Built a user-friendly laundry app focused on transparency and convenience to improve overall satisfaction.
  2. Enabled users to upload clothing photos and add special instructions for personalized, damage-free handling.
  3. Added a clear, upfront price summary before users proceed to payment.
  4. Integrated real-time updates and a progress bar so users can track their laundry at every step.
  5. Offered flexible service options, including standard and express delivery to suit different schedules.
  6. Implemented order history, spending summaries, and service rating tools for transparency and user feedback.
