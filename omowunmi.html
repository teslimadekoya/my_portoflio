OMOWUNMI'S KITCHEN

2025
Fawaz Adewuyi
3 weeks

Project Overview

Omowunmi’s Kitchen is a catering and rentals brand in Nigeria, offering food services and event rentals. The goal of this project was to create a simple, modern website that builds trust, communicates services clearly, and drives users to WhatsApp for bookings.

VISIT WEBSITE

The Problem

The business had no digital presence. Customers only discovered them through word of mouth, which made it difficult to scale. They needed a professional site that presented their services with clarity and provided a direct way for potential clients to reach them.

Project Goal

Design a mobile experience that directly addresses the trust, communication, and convenience gaps in Nigeria's laundry market. The aim is an app that feels dependable and personal, from scheduling to delivery.

Research & Insights

I studied competitor catering and rental websites. Insights showed that users value clear service offerings, photos of past work, pricing transparency where possible, and an easy contact method. WhatsApp was chosen because it is familiar, fast, and direct for Nigerian customers.

Design Strategy

The design focused on clarity and simplicity. Navigation was kept minimal with Home, Menu, About, Rentals, and Contact. Large, high-quality images were used to showcase catering and rental setups.

Design Process

I began with sketches and wireframes, then built a responsive layout using components and variables. Trust elements such as, customer photos, photos of past events and clear contact information were added. The visual design leaned on showcasing imagery since in services like this require more showing and less telling to gain customer's trust.

Final Website

The final website featured a striking hero section with a headline and supporting text to introduce the brand. A fixed header included the logo, navigation links for Home, Menu, Rentals, About, and a clear Contact button for easy access. Scrolling down revealed the menu offerings including Intercontinental dishes, African cuisine, and cakes. The rentals section highlighted catering, furniture, and decoration services, while the about page included a short write up and an inside look at Omowunmi’s kitchen with real images. Every page ended with a call to action through the contact button, and WhatsApp integration made communication quick and direct. The design leaned heavily on imagery, as showing rather than telling was the best way to reassure users of quality and build trust.

Outcome & Reflection

The website gave Omowunmi’s Kitchen a professional digital presence and made it easier for customers to connect. Direct WhatsApp contact was used as the main CTA because catering and rental services often involve details and intricacies that are best discussed directly. The design reflects trust, quality, and professionalism while showing the breadth of services through images.
